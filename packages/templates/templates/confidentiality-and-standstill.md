# Confidentiality and Standstill Agreement

This Confidentiality and Standstill Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of {{effective_date}} between {{buyer_name}} ("Buyer") and {{target_name}} ("Target").

## 1. Confidentiality

Buyer agrees to keep all non-public information received about Target confidential and to use it solely for the purpose of evaluating a potential acquisition.

## 2. Standstill

For a period of {{standstill_period_months}} months from {{effective_date}}, Buyer agrees not to, without Target's prior written consent:

- Acquire or offer to acquire any securities or assets of Target;
- Propose any merger, tender offer, or other business combination involving Target;
- Solicit proxies or seek to influence the management or board of Target.

## 3. Term

The confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement; the standstill obligations expire at the end of the standstill period stated above.
